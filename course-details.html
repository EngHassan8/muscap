5–6 Week Public Speaking Course — Group Class

Barnaamijkan waa group class xirfad sare leh oo loogu talagalay dadka raba inay ka gudbaan cabsida fagaare ka hadalka, kana dhisaan kalsooni dhab ah oo la dareemi karo.

Mudada 5–6 toddobaad, waxaad baran doontaa farsamooyin kaa dhigaya qof si cad, kalsooni leh, una hadla si saameyn ku reebta dadka hortooda.

Waxaad baran doontaa:

  • Sida loo xakameeyo cabsi & walwal
  • Sida loo diyaariyo khudbad professional ah
  • Sida loo qaabeeyo hadalka (structure & flow)
  • Sida loo hadlo si saameyn leh
  • Body language & eye contact
  • Cod isticmaal & kalsooni dhisid

Yaa loogu talagalay?

  • Arday jaamacadeed
  • Xirfadlayaal shaqo
  • Ganacsato & leaders
  • Qof kasta oo raba horumar shaqsiyeed

Sida uu u socdo:

  • 5–6 toddobaad
  • Group Class (koox yar)
  • Tababar ficil ah
  • Feedback & guidance toos ah

Qiimaha: La xiriir si faahfaahin loo helo
